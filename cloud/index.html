FROM OBJECT
TO TRUTH STATE
THE ALETHRA™ FLOW

STEP 1 — CAPTURE

ALETHRA SENSE™ and ALETHRA SCAN™ observe the item using multi-sensor and micro-surface analysis. The system captures material signatures, surface texture, and environmental context.

STEP 2 — CONFIRM

Signals are fused and checked against prior verifications where relevant. ALETHRA ENV™ adds situational intelligence: storage conditions, handling anomalies, and risk indicators.

STEP 3 — CERTIFY

ALETHRA CLOUD™ records a new or updated truth state for the item: identity, continuity, integrity, and a trust score derived from sensor evidence and policy rules.

STEP 4 — CONNECT

Cloud platforms, AI agents, and enterprise systems consume this truth state via APIs or event streams. They can enforce rules such as “only act when trust score > threshold” or “flag any continuity break.”
