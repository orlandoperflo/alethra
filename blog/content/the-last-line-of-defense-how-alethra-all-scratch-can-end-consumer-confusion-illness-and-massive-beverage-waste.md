- __Millions of safe bottles were destroyed__ because manufacturers couldn’t verify which were contaminated and which were not.  

- __Thousands of consumers became sick__ from hidden allergens, mislabeled sugar levels, cross-contamination, or counterfeit beverages.  

- __Entire product lines were recalled__ because distributors mixed pallets and retailers couldn’t isolate the risk.  

- __Billions of plastic bottles and aluminum cans__ were dumped into landfills during recall events triggered by pure *confusion*, not danger.  

- Many consumers unknowingly consumed unsafe or temperature-abused beverages from unverified sellers online.

This is the reality: __Confusion destroys more beverages than contamination ever will — and it harms more people, too.__
