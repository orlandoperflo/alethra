A patented specialty ink that lets anyone scratch their name or identifying mark directly onto disposable packaging — eliminating “whose is whose” in real time.

A PATENTED INK THAT ELIMINATES CONFUSION

All-Scratch!® is a friction-activated ink engineered for bottles, cups, cans, cartons, and other disposable packaging. With nothing more than a fingernail, key, ring, or coin, users can scratch their name, initials, or symbol directly into the ink layer — creating a permanent, unmistakable identifier.

It turns every package into a personalized, confusion-proof item.

SIMPLE. PERMANENT. IMPOSSIBLE TO CONFUSE.

SCRATCH TO REVEAL

The ink layer activates with friction, exposing a bright permanent mark instantly.

NO MARKERS OR STICKERS NEEDED

It works anytime, anywhere — no tools required. The package itself is the tool.
