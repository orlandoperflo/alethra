Construction rework typically consumes:

  • 5%–10% of the total project cost on well-managed sites
  • 10%–20% or more on complex or poorly coordinated projects
  • Extreme cases exceeding 30%

Using a $100 million project as a baseline:

  • • 5% rework = $5 million lost
  • • 10% rework = $10 million lost
  • • 20% rework = $20 million lost

And half of that rework traces back to one issue: communication and information breakdowns.

2.4 Miscommunication: Nearly 50% of All Rework

Studies show that up to 48% of construction rework stems from poor communication or inaccurate project information.

That’s billions of dollars in lost revenue because teams lacked clarity.

3. ROOT CAUSES OF CONFUSION
& WASTE

Construction’s confusion crisis stems from four core failure zones:

3.1 Bad or Incomplete Data

Insufficient data is not a technical glitch — it is operational misalignment.

This includes:

  • Outdated plans
  • Mislabeled materials
  • Incorrect specifications
  • Unverified deliveries
  • Inconsistent safety protocols
  • Undocumented changes
  • Missing records

When the data is wrong, everything downstream is wrong.

3.2 Materials Misidentification & Fraud

  • Material substitution is a quiet epidemic in construction.
  • Cheaper materials get swapped for higher-margin ones.
  • Suppliers send incorrect grades.
  • Labels get misprinted.
  • The wrong items get installed.

The industry has no real-time verification layer.

3.3 Communication Breakdowns

Construction sites are tangled communication webs:

  • Trades talking over each other
  • Supervisors relying on verbal updates
  • Workers missing briefings
  • Inspectors showing up late
  • Suppliers misjudging timelines

One missed message becomes a week of rework.

3.4 Lack of Real-Time Verification

  • The industry relies on “trust-based workflows.”
  • Trust the shipment is correct.
  • Trust the panel is fire-rated.
  • Trust the worker is certified.
  • Trust the installation meets code.

This trust-based culture is what creates trillion-dollar waste.

ALETHRA™ replaces trust with verification.
