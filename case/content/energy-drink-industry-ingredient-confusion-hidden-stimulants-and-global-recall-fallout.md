# __The Incident__
One of the world’s fastest-growing energy drink companies experienced an explosive crisis after multiple production runs contained __undisclosed stimulant compounds__ far exceeding label claims.
Here’s what triggered the meltdown:
- A supplier delivered caffeine base with an incorrect concentration — 3x higher than the spec sheet.
- Flavored versions used different ingredient decks, but the co-packer applied the same base mixture across all SKUs due to a mislabeled barrel.
- Batch codes printed at Plant A and Plant B weren’t aligned; distributors couldn’t tell which cans came from which line.
- Barcode scanners at the packaging line routed the wrong can sleeves — making sugar-free cans display incorrect ingredient panels.
- Retailers reported customer health incidents, forcing mandatory regulatory intervention.
In just 48 hours, the brand went from viral success to national liability.
